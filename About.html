About Lake Shelbyville

Welcome to Lake Shelbyville, your premier destination for outdoor recreation in the heart of Central Illinois. Formed by the damming of the Kaskaskia River, this 11,100-acre lake offers unparalleled opportunities for boating, fishing, swimming, and camping.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a safe, enjoyable, and scenic environment for visitors to connect with nature. Whether you are looking for a weekend getaway or an adventurous fishing trip, Lake Shelbyville offers numerous recreation areas, including sprawling sandy beaches, hiking trails, and picturesque picnic spots.
